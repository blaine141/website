I.R.I.S.

December 2014 - February 2016

    This is a Visual Basic program that monitors a Gmail account that I text from my "dumb phone" using mail.dll. I.R.I.S. then runs it through a list of commands to find what I am asking, then responds with an appropriate response. This not only works with phones, but with normal emails too.

    Examples of these commands are:

  • What are my grades?
  • What homework do I have?
  • What does barbarous mean?
  • Is Ollie's open? (Ollie's is an Ice Cream Shop)
  • Turn on my lamp.
  • What is pi?
  • Tell me a joke.
  • (5/7)^2/3+19/14


    Phone smartener also has the ability to notify me when these events happen:

  • I receive an e-mail.
  • My GPA changes.
  • Someone turns on my lamp.
  • I have homework due today.
